## Biography
Timothy Theodore Duncan is widely considered the greatest power forward in NBA history. Born in St. Croix, US Virgin Islands in 1976, he was an exceptional swimmer before switching to basketball at age 14 and developing into the top pick in the 1997 NBA Draft. He spent 19 seasons with the San Antonio Spurs, winning five NBA championships, earning 19 All-NBA selections and 15 All-Defensive team selections. His partnership with coach Gregg Popovich produced the most successful coach-player relationship in NBA history.

## Accomplishments Timeline
- **2014** Last Championship — Beautiful Basketball: Led the Spurs to their most beautiful championship, dismantling the Miami Heat with the most aesthetically pleasing basketball in NBA history.
