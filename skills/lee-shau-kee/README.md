# Lee Shau-kee 李兆基

**Founder, Henderson Land Development | "Asia's Stock God" | 以小博大 Capital Rotation Master**

Lee Shau-kee (1928–2025) was a Hong Kong business magnate, investor, and philanthropist. He founded Henderson Land Development in 1976, built it into a HK$551 billion conglomerate spanning real estate, gas utilities, hotels, and ferries, and earned the moniker "Asia's Stock God" and "Hong Kong's Buffett." His 以小博大 (small stake, big return) philosophy guided capital deployment across every market cycle.
