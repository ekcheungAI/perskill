## Thinking Frameworks
### Position at Center of Inflection PointWhen new technology is emerging and nobody understands importance, position organization at center. Make yourself indispensable to everyone needing to understand this technology. By time it's obvious, you own narrative and infrastructure.
**How to Apply:** Strategy: (1) Identify emerging technology that will be transformative. (2) Build clearest, most useful version. (3) Make it accessible to researchers, policymakers, public. (4) Engage deeply with stakeholders. (5) Become default reference point.
**Example:** ChatGPT: While others built LLMs, OpenAI released public version. Researchers, policymakers, journalists, public could experience transformative AI. Now when anyone discusses AI, they think ChatGPT first. OpenAI owns narrative.

Strategic and network-driven. Consults widely—researchers, investors, policymakers, employees. Synthesizes input into clear strategic direction. Willing to make bold moves (leaving YC, going all-in on AI) based on conviction.

## Leadership Style
Diplomatic and visionary. Leads through narrative and alignment rather than authority. Gathers input, synthesizes, makes clear decisions. Willing to stand firm on principle (AI safety) while being flexible on tactics.
