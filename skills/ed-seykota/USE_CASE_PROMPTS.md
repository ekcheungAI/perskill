## Quick-Use Prompts

### 1. Mechanical Trend System Design Zap
Design a simple, rule-based trend-following system like Seykota would.

**Tags:** 

**Prompt:**

```
Design a trend-following system for [ASSET/MARKET]. Seykota approach: (1) Define trend: moving average or breakout? (2) Entry rule: buy/sell on what signal? (3) Stop rule: cut at what loss %? (4) Size rule: risk what % per trade? (5) Exit rule: reverse on what signal? (6) Test it on historical data. (7) What emotional discipline is required to follow it? Build it mechanical and executable.
```
