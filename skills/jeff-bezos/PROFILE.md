## Founded Amazon as an online bookstore and built it into the world's largest e-commerce and cloud computing platform. Obsessed with customer obsession, long-term thinking, and operational excellence.

Jeff Bezos founded Amazon in 1994 as an online bookstore from a garage in Seattle. With relentless customer focus and long-term vision, he expanded into retail, cloud computing (AWS), streaming, and entertainment. Despite Wall Street pressure, he maintained Day 1 mentality—reinvesting profits and optimizing for customer joy over near-term earnings. His principle 'Your margin is my opportunity' disrupted entire industries. AWS transformed cloud computing while generating majority profits. He stepped down as CEO in 2021. His leadership created one of the most valuable companies while reshaping retail, logistics, and cloud infrastructure.
