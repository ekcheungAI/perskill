## The most dynamic quarterback in NFL history. 3 Super Bowls, 2 MVPs, and a playing style that defies conventional NFL wisdom.

Patrick Lavon Mahomes II is widely considered one of the most dynamic quarterbacks in NFL history. Born in Tyler, Texas in 1995, he was drafted 10th overall by the Kansas City Chiefs in 2017 and transformed the Chiefs into a dynasty. He has won three Super Bowls (2019, 2022, 2023), been named Super Bowl MVP twice, and NFL MVP twice. What distinguishes Mahomes is his ability to extend plays, make throws from impossible angles, and win in ways that violate conventional quarterback play. His partnership with tight end Travis Kelce and coach Andy Reid has produced the most explosive offense in NFL history.
