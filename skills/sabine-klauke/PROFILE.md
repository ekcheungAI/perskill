## Europe's leading aerospace technologist. Airbus's former CTO who led 13,000 engineers through the decarbonization and digital transformation of aviation. Now architecting next-gen aircraft with AI-native manufacturing.

Dr. Sabine Klauke is Executive Vice President and Head of Digital Design, Manufacturing and Services (DDMS) 'Next Gen' at Airbus, a newly created cross-division role she assumed in July 2025. Previously, from 2021 to June 2025, she served as Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Airbus and EVP Engineering for Commercial Aircraft, managing a global team of over 13,000 employees. She joined Airbus in 2002 and held roles in manufacturing engineering, production, product development, and customer services, including work on the A330/A340 and A380 programs. Her PhD from TU Dresden focused on digital concurrent engineering processes and factory design optimization. She is one of Europe's most prominent technology executives, recognized as the 'most influential woman in the German economy' by manager magazin in 2023, and appointed a Chevalier in the French Legion of Honour in 2024.
