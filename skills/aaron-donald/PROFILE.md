## The most dominant defensive player in NFL history. 10 consecutive Pro Bowls, 3 Defensive Player of the Year awards, and a Super Bowl champion. The most unstoppable defensive force of his generation.

Aaron Donald is widely considered the most dominant defensive player in NFL history. Born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania in 1991, he was drafted 13th overall by the St. Louis Rams in 2014 and spent 10 seasons with the Rams. He was selected to 10 consecutive Pro Bowls, 10 First-Team All-Pro selections (a record for defensive tackles), and won three NFL Defensive Player of the Year awards — the most ever by a defensive tackle. His combination of explosive power, speed, and technical skill was unprecedented. His one missing achievement — a Super Bowl — was achieved in 2022 when he dominated the Bengals' offensive line in the final minutes of Super Bowl LVI.
