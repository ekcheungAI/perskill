## Full Biography

Kathryn Grace Ledecky is widely considered the greatest female swimmer in history. Born in Washington D.C. in 1997, she was a 15-year-old high school student when she won her first Olympic gold at Rio 2016, setting an American record in the 800m freestyle. She competes in distances from 200m to 1500m — a range that requires radically different strategies — making her dominance across all distances even more remarkable. She has won 10 Olympic medals (7 gold) and 21 World Championship gold medals — more than any female swimmer in history.
