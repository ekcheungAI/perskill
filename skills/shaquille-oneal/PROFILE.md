## The most physically dominant basketball player of his era. 4 championships, 3 Finals MVPs, and a personality that made him one of the most beloved athletes in sports history.

Shaquille Rashaun O'Neal is widely considered one of the most physically dominant players in NBA history. Born in Newark, New Jersey in 1972, he was 7'1", 325 pounds, with speed and coordination that were unprecedented for his size. Drafted first overall by the Orlando Magic in 1992, he won four NBA championships (three with the Lakers, one with the Heat), was named Finals MVP three times, and was selected to 15 All-Star Games. After retiring, he became a TNT analyst, rapper, actor, and businessman. His physical dominance combined with an outsized personality to create one of the most complete sports legacies in history.
